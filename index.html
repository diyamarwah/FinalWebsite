Design Justification

Further Design Elements

In the entirety of the website, there is a common theme of a blue and yellow pallette. This is in tandem with the colours of the logo. Like any other business, charities also have contemporaries that may do a similar task that they undertake.

With a constant colour theme, the brand is reflected so people remember careFlight and the work it does and can most importantly recognise it in any situation.

The "Poppins" font used helped convey a relaxed website with no trappings of a conventional, formal and old school typography.
